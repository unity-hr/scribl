Information we collect

We store your Device token to be able to send push notifications. We also store your Name and Facebook ID so You're able to send and receive messages.

We DO NOT read or use your messages in any way other than making communication between you and your friends possible.

Transparency

We are open to your suggestions and will disclose any matter that won't harm any of the users or any 3rd party being used in order to provide the service of communication. For any suggestions and questions please contact us at support@scriblapp.com
